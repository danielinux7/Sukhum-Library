          Before 1938, the library fund acquisition was not of a routine nature; Circulation and readership were insignificant. For example, in 1935, the library book fund, was 21,5tys. copies, and readers served 1975 people. Mass work is almost not carried out, was not provided and methodological assistance to other institutions of the republic library. Qualification Central Library staff has been very low. The radical improvement of the situation in Abkhazia librarianship occurred in 1935. August 28 made a Resolution of the Council of People's Commissars №65 «On the reorganization of the Sukhumi city library Republican; with the addition thereto of the Sukhumi City Reading Room. 26 Baku commissars. " Approved was the estimate of the Republican library to 31 December 1938 in the amount of 54.1 thousand. Rubles. Since then, the library operation in Abkhazia is greatly improved. Doubled the number of readers; half - delivery of books. Rose book collection. Republican State Library has become composed of 15 employees. However, with the library it remained unsuitable cramped basement, almost unusable. Only July 2, 1942 according to the Decree of People's Commissars of Abkhazia, on the order of republican Narkomprossu library was put to the first floor of a former boarding school building №16. The next phase of revitalization of library development of Abkhazia begins after the end of World War II. In 1945, two and a half times increase the budget of the Republican library book fund increases, the number of readers reaches the pre-war level, is activated to provide guidance to the library institutions of Abkhazia.

          Over the next 5 years of the republican budget of the library has almost doubled and amounted 236,8tys. rubles, book collection added to 77,7tys.ekzemplyarov; and a half-fold increase in the number of readers; two - money for purchase of literature. In 1953 and 1956gg. library book fund significantly increased at the expense of the big parties, literature, received a gift from the USSR State Library. Lenin.

          I changed and the level of skills of library staff, if in 1950 in Abkhazia was only one person with a higher education library; in 1960 these employees were already six.

          In connection with all the positive changes it became necessary to build a special, model building, and that was done in 1980. The library departments were organized: printed music literature; technical and agricultural literature; literature in foreign languages ​​department, department of mass work, and others.

          February 17, 1981 Decision of the Council of Ministers of the Abkhaz ASSR Republican Library was named the People's Writer of Abkhazia Petrovskii Papaskiri.

          In subsequent years, the activities of the library is constantly improved; increased book collection, increased the number of readers, organized perfected new forms of work. Everything was interrupted August 14, 1992 invasion of the Georgian State Council troops in Abkhazia. During the occupation of Georgian library building received significant damage.

A significant part of the book fund was destroyed by fire, damaged and inoperable due to flooding of the premises stacks, simply looted. After the victory in the Patriotic War of the Abkhaz people had to start from scratch. Revival Library. I.G.Papaskir largely due to the arrival of a new director B.Sh.Cholariya, managed to organize the rest of the team and new employees to work to save the surviving post-war library property. At the cost of enormous effort that called on "naked entuziatsii" a small team headed by the Director has done the hard work of recovery of flooded basements of several thousand books, according to their restoration, cleaning and repair of damaged buildings and equipment of war. The most difficult time for Abkhazia, during the post-war chaos and the economic blockade of the republic staff of the library was able to as soon as possible to resume its activities; as far as possible, to restore the preserved book fund; undertake renovation of premises. Director of the Library, with the support team carried out extensive work on exploring possibilities of restoration and replenishment of the book fund. It has also been established and reached agreement with the library institutions of the Russian Federation, international organizations, various non-governmental institutions for assistance in ensuring its library literature, inventories and equipment. Moreover, continuously carried out, and carried out the process of implementation and development of new library technologies, forms and methods of work, contributing to better meet reader demand. Parallel to this, a lot of attention is paid to the training of library staff. Whenever possible, the management of the library fails to organize the participation of representatives of Abkhazia library institutions in various international forums and conferences library. Changed the name, and the status of the library: November 27, 1998 the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Abkhazia was assigned the status of "National" and later it was decided to call "The National Library of the Republic of Abkhazia of Ivan G. Papaskiri". Speaking about today's library, it should be noted that along with the readers of maintenance activities to a great place in its work is paid, above all, the development of national and local literature, for which public sector organized bibliography. With regard to better meet reader demand; in the absence of a centralized this problem must be solved through direct procurement of the relevant literature in the bookselling facilities Sochi. Moreover, in conditions of insufficient funding from the state, library management constantly for these purposes other possibilities. Assistance in the acquisition of book fund and library of the Russian Federation provided the institution. With all the difficulties about which more will be mentioned; the National Library of great attention is paid to the restructuring of the system of library activities, the development and application of new library technologies. Guidelines Library succeeded in organizing a new department to which the relevant equipment was purchased; "IRBIS" to master the program. Who is working on the creation of electronic databases; wider implementation of modern technologies in the work of the library.

          Currently, the National Library of the Republic of Abkhazia actually performs the functions of the main library in the country; scientific-methodical center of library work; State Book Chamber of Abkhazia. Much attention is paid to providing guidance to other agencies of the library, you are working on the organization of compliance with international standards in the production process in the Republic of printed products, sending it to the appropriate institutions of countries near and far abroad, the delivery of the Russian Federation in Abkhazia periodic publications.
